What is Devconnect?

Devconnect is the first Ethereum World’s Fair: a week-long gathering of the Ethereum community that brings together experts, builders, and researchers to dive deep into specific topics through independently hosted events.

What is Ethereum Day?

Ethereum Day is the one-day opening event of Devconnect, hosted by the Ethereum Foundation. It highlights key updates, research, and initiatives shaping the future of Ethereum.

Who can attend Ethereum Day?

Anyone interested in blockchain technology, decentralized systems, or the Ethereum ecosystem — from newcomers to experienced developers.

Why should I attend or participate?

To meet leading voices in Ethereum, exchange ideas, and get inspired by projects shaping the next era of decentralized technology.

When and where does Ethereum Day take place?

Ethereum Day takes place on November 17, 2025, from 9 AM to 7 PM, at La Rural, Buenos Aires — the same venue that will host other Devconnect events during the week.
